ABOUT ME

Design is also struggle, fighting for one's ideals. Albe Steiner struggled to leave a positive mark that could influence all designers who would come after. His teaching and struggle for the social using graphic design taught me to relate to design not only in a cold and detached way. For this reason, I have done numerous social design projects over the past 10 years.

I LOVE DESIGNING BRANDING

There is no design without logos and brands. In a world increasingly involved with social network, we often forget how powerful a logo can be. I love designing visual identities and am always looking for new branding projects.
